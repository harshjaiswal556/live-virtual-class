Terms & Conditions

  • Check the 10th and 12th Grade Percentages: Look at the percentage scores obtained in both the 10th and 12th grades.
  • Evaluate the First Condition: If both the 10th and 12th grade percentages are greater than or equal to 90%, then: Award a 50% Scholarship.
  • Evaluate the Second Condition: If both the 10th and 12th grade percentages are greater than or equal to 80% but less than 90%, then: Award a 25% Scholarship.
  • Evaluate the Else Condition: If the percentages do not meet either of the above conditions, then: No Scholarship is awarded.
